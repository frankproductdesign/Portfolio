Me



I’m a multidisciplinary Senior UX designer currently living in Ottawa, Canada with my wife and two kids. Over the years I have worked with companies to help them connect with their customers through UX research, user centered design and collaboration. Perpetually curious, passionate about UX, with 15+ years of experience as a designer, I have a background as a graphic designer creating visual assets for different digital products. I have collaborated with teams designing and developing B2B web applications, e-commerce websites, games, and mobile apps.

I work well with product managers, designers, developers, and other stakeholders evaluating user needs, defining design strategies and implementing meaningful design experiences.




Skills


User Experience design, Interaction design, User-centered design, Agile user stories, Wire-framing, Mobile UX design (iOS, Android), Prototyping (Adobe CC; XD, Illustrator, Photoshop. Sketch). HTML, CSS (Sass), Javascript, frameworks; AngularJS, JQuery. Sublime text, Git, Github, Terminal. Agile development. User research (concept and requirements validation, evaluation, focus groups).




Process



Discovery


Define the problem - that needs a solution. Kick-off meeting, requirements, specifications.



Research


User research, personas, surveys, interviews, ethnography, contextual research, competitive analysis.



Architecture


Synthesize information, build share understanding - journey maps, mental models, user stories. Affinity diagram, taxonomy, card sorting.



Prototyping and Testing


Sketch multiple solutions, get early feedback and refine. Wireframes. Build interactive prototypes. A/B testing. Create visual designs - concepts, stylescapes, design systems.



Validate and Iterate


Collect feedback - Evaluate test results and determine necessary modifications.
