E-commerce website


Olive and Well is a Gourmet Foods Tasting Shop specializing in the most flavorful Extra-Virgin Olive Oils and Balsamic Vinegars from around the world. Their physical store is located in Oak Park, Chicago, USA.

At the store, customers are able to taste their premium Olive Oils and Balsamic Vinegars before making their selection. Oils and vinegars are bottled from the same Italian Fusti (stainless steel tank) that they sample at the store.
For more information visit oliveandwell.com

Responsive design

UI redesign - case study. The existing version of this website is one of the few cases where I have done the entire front-end coding (in AngularJS). Server side and shopping-cart functionality coded by Iroel Perez.

Modules


Using a mobile first approach, a module was created for each product to display its information throghout all the different screen resolutions.

On small screens only the name of the product is displayed. The purpose is to minimize vertical scrolling. On tap the section displaying the product expands to show more information.
