Process



Research and Set Goals


Before jumping into the design of the project, competitive and user-centered research is done to understand the target audience. Determine the goals of the project, and define a design direction.



Sketch and Prototype


Sketch as many solutions as possible, select a few and refine. Depending on the scale and type of project, create prototypes in Adobe XD or Sketch, and prototype the design in code in the case of websites.



Test and Collect


If a prototype can convey the design solution’s intent, users can test it. Gather user feedback and translate these insights into new solutions.



Simplify and Iterate


Evaluate test results and iterate. Post-launch A/B tests and analytics determine further refinement needs.
