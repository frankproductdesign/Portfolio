About



I’m a UX/UI designer located in Ottawa, Canada. Over the years I have worked with companies to help them connect with their customers through UX research, user centered design and collaboration. Perpetually curious, passionate about UX, I have collaborated with teams designing and developing a range of web applications, e-commerce websites, games, and mobile apps.

User Experience design, Interaction design, User-centered design, Wire-framing, App design (iOS, Android), Prototyping (mostly Sketch, and Adobe XD). HTML, CSS, Javascript, frameworks; Sass, Less, AngularJS. Agile development. User research (concept requirements, validation and evaluation). Concepts, user flow creation, UI design, follow up through development.
