GreenSpace

GreenSpace was a social network game (for Facebook) created to promote environmentalism. The game follows the freemium model of game monetization with in-app purchases.
Gameplay; players select pieces of trash for their GreenSpace Corps avatar to clear. Energy is required to clear trash off the planet. Players level-up by collecting experience points. Large pieces of garbage require the player to reach higher level before they can be cleared. Players can also gain experience by completing missions.

Awards and recognition
