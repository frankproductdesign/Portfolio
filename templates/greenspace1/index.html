Social Network Game

Environments

Floating islands were illustrated representing different areas of the planet, they were accessible as the players progressed in the game. The area of the surface was calculated using a grid. Each square in the grid contained another grid of 9x9 squares. One of these squares was the equivalent to the size of the main character.

Trees

Some areas on the islands had spots with tree fossils. These could be respawn and grown into adult trees. On adult stage trees attract different species (NPCs) that help the player to collect materials and currency needed in the game. Other trees were grown or were discover through different mechanics of the game.

Other trees were given to players through contests and special missions, or were available in the game store.
